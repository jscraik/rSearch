## Our standards
We are committed to a harassment-free experience for everyone. Please:
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical merit.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying.
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances.
- Trolling or sustained disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
If you experience or witness a violation, contact the maintainers:
- Email: jscraik@brainwav.io

Maintainers may remove content, lock threads, or block contributors as needed.

## Scope
This policy applies to all project spaces including issues, pull requests, and discussions.
